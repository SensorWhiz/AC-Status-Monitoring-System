Sensor connection
Connect The Aout pin of the WCS1700 sensor to A0 pin of Arduino.
Please note that Arduino Nano does not support the Library 'WinsonLib.h>'.
Connect the Vcc and Gnd pins of WCS1700 to 5V amd Gnd on the Arduino respectively.

For Serial communication between Arduino and Nodemcu
Connect D0 on Nodemcu to Pin 6 on Arduino and D1 on Nodemcu to Pin 7 on Arduino.
